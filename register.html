How to Apply?

Better Understanding of Better World Indonesia open two different ways of registration, which are Call for Application and Call for Paper. Participants from Call for Application will need to submit online application only. Meanwhile, participants have responsibility to pay all accommodation during the conference and transportation from home to Yogyakarta. There will be no registration fee. If participants apply for Paper , they must submit online application form and paper related with our theme. Five best papers will be invited to the conference and present their ideas to BUBW conference. Chosen participants will not need to pay fee for accommodation during in Yogyakarta. Meanwhile, transportation from home to Yogyakarta will be self-funded by the participants.



Registration Flow

Call for Application

  1. Download the Aplication Form in our website, bubwindonesia.com
  2. Fill in the Application Form
  3. Print this form after you fill in its entirety, and sign it with written signature
  4. Send a color scanned document of this form to: bubwindonesia@gmail.com
  5. Participants will receive the email confirmation from the BUBW Indonesia Committee at maximum 48 hours after we received the email.
  6. Committee will send the Letter of Acceptance and the document requirement
  7. In the Letter of Acceptance, there will be Bank Account to transfer the fee of BUBW Indonesia. Fee rate will be published in advance.
  8. After you completely transfer the fee, we will give you an Invitation Letter to state that you are officially accepted as BUBW Indonesia participant.

Call for Paper

  1. Participants must pay the registration fee for each paper to BUBW account
    • National participant: IDR 300,000
    • International participant: USD 30
    • BUBW Account
  2. Download the Aplication Form in our website, bubwindonesia.com
  3. Fill in the application form
  4. Print this form after you fill in its entirety, and sign it with written signature
  5. Paper requirements:
  6. Submit the scanned colored application, paper, and payment receipt to this email bubwindonesia@gmail.com
  7. Fifteen best papers will be chosen and invited to BUBW conference in Yogyakarta, Indonesia
  8. Semifinalists must prepare a presentation about the papers (videos, photos, or other artifacts) to be delivered in the conference*
  9. Rate fee for Call for Paper participants will be published in advance
  10. There will be certificates and award categories for the winners


Eligibility

  1. The participants are expected to be 18-30 years old highly motivated individual, who demonstrate leadership through academic work, community involvement and extracurricular activities.
  2. Participants can be from any region in Indonesia and other country.
  3. Proficient in English.
  4. The participants should have a strong interest in interfaith and global understanding, pluralism, global citizenship, conflict resolution, and sustainable development.
  5. The participant are suggested to have a proven track record of previous and current activities on interfaith and global understanding, pluralism, global citizenship, conflict resolution, and sustainable development.
  6. Indicate a serious interest in learning about Indonesia with its pluralism.
  7. Be mature, responsible, independent, confident, open-minded, tolerant, thoughtful and inquisitive.
  8. Be willing and able to fully participate in the Better Understanding for Better World 2015 program.
  9. Be comfortable and prepared to share living accommodations, able to make adjustment to cultural and social practices different from those of their home country.
  10. International participants are highly recommended to have passports


Details of Payment

You have to send us the digital copy of payment no longer than 10 days after you receive the Letter of Acceptance. Better Understanding for Better World (BUBW) Indonesia Fee is 300 USD.

*Fee includes:

  • BUBW activities and programs
  • BUBW merchandises
  • Tour, interfaith, and cultural programs
  • 5 days 4 nights stay at hotel
  • Transportation (arrival from airport to hotel, hotel to venue, any transportation needed during arranged programs, and departure from hotel to airport)
  • All times daily meals
  • Coffee break

*Fee does not includes:

  • Health insurance
  • Visa and travel
  • Bank transfer and/or correspondent bank charges
  • Souvenirs and shopping activities
  • Any additional charge that does not related with BUBW program

Details of Payment

  1. After you accept Letter of Acceptance, you will be given bank account of BUBW Indonesia, then you have to pay BUBW Indonesia fee. Rate will be published in advance.
  2. After you pay the fee, inform us via bubwindonesia@gmail.com immediately.
  3. Scan the Application for Fund Transfer / Proof of Payment (the paper you get from bank as the proof that you’ve done the payment to BUBW Indonesia’s bank account number).
  4. If you do not send the scanning in 10 days, we will remind you to send it in the next 5 days.
  5. If after those 5 days you still do not send us your Application for Fund Transfer / Proof of Payment via email, we will list your name as waiting list for BUBW Indonesia.
  6. If you’ve transferred the fee but you cancel your application as participant of BUBW Indonesia, there will be NO REFUND.
